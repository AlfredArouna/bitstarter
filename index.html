SMS are use in many situation. From professionnal context to frendship context. Base on ... Study, user check they phone 23 time a day for messaging, making messaging the most time cunsummable activity.

But, SMS still not organize as mail are in a conversation way. By SMS we can discuss a long, long time about a topic. You SMS/MMS inbox did not organise your SMS/MMS flow as a conversation.

Smart SMS Conversation is a mobile application that organise your SMS messages in a professional way by grouping them in conversation. With Smart SMS Conversation, you can follow your exchange with your contacts when you're texting.

Features

  • Smart Ordering (unread SMS)
  • Conversation list grouped by thread
  • Conversation list grouped by recipents
  • Conversation list grouped by date and time
  • Human readable date of week (e.g., 'yesterday' instead of the full date)
  • Search your SMS inbox.
  • Filter your search result.

FAQ

What is Conversation?
Conversation enable SMS flow organisation in an human conversation way, as in in mail box using mail user agent. With Conversation, your SMS/MMS are organised by thread.
What's a thread?
In Conversation, a thread is the ten firsts characters of a new SMS. Each time an user reply to an other SMS with was a relpy for this original SMS or reply to the original SMS, we put them in the same conversation.
Can i change default organization?
Of course, you cas use filter to reorganize your SMS Inbox. By default, SMS are organized by thread. For the moment, filters are:
  • Thread (subject of SMS)
  • Date and Time of SMS
  • Recepients of SMS
Can i search SMS ?
We plan to include search in SMS Inbox. For each serach result, you can apply existing filters.
How to use Conversation ?
Two steps:
  • Download and install Conversation application
  • Lunch Conversation to manage your SMS
